🔬 Methodology Overview

🧪 Feature Extraction

Analyzes protein-RNA complexes by computing center-of-mass distances between amino acid residues and nucleotides, along with hydrogen bond counts using a 3.5 Å distance threshold.

🤖 Machine Learning

Random Forest regressor trained on binding free energy decomposition data predicts interaction strength using geometric descriptors and chemical properties.

📈 Score Interpretation

Negative scores indicate favorable binding interactions, while positive scores suggest unfavorable or weakly binding complexes. Magnitude represents binding strength.
